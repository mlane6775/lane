    15 Best Stock Trading Apps For Android & iOS

    banking and finance apps, Mobile Apps
    Posted on Thursday July 30, 2020

    Stock trading is now easier with mobile apps, each stock trading app has a design interface developed in a way that it is active on multiple screens. All stages of a full-fledged trading practice are composed intuitively into a stock trading app.

    The COVID pandemic has made a gripping effect on every sector, especially on financial management. These apps can help both beginners and seasoned traders to practice regular trading activities without physical efforts.

    Below listed are the best 15 stock trading apps you can rely on for matchless trading & financial services.

    Tradingview Fidelity Investments Trading212
    Acorns Guru Trade7 Capital.com
    Olymp Trade RobinHood Groww
    GOFOREX StockEdge Kite by Zerodha
    Exness Trader FBS Copytrade Schwab Mobile

     

    1. Tradingview

    Tradingview is a simple app with extensive reach to stock markets. You can have effective tracking on trading & major global indices via the app. Both first time traders & professional technical analysts can use this app to publish & view their trading ideas.

    Tradingview enables you to connect with traders from different regions and discuss awesome trading ideas with them. The app gives you real-time access to global expenses, gives you a chance to have your watchlist & get alerts on subjects of your choice.

    App Features

    • Advanced charts for market Analysis.
    • Auto-syncing your accounts.
    • Real-time tracking of commodity prices
    • Opportunity to gauge data from global exchanges.

    Ratings: 4.6/5

    Installs: 1,000,000+

    Download: Android, iOS

    2. Fidelity Investments

    Fidelity Investments is much popular for its convenient mobile trading platform for the last 70 years. You can install the app, start your trade, & pay all your bills all in a few taps.

    Fidelity Investments actively manages accounts by knowing details on transactions & other activities in your account. You get customized feeds about your deposits & interests with very simplified navigation.

    App Features

    • In-depth information on your watchlist
    • Insights on international markets
    • Timely push notifications & alerts
    • Instant feedback from other representatives.

    Ratings:4.6/5

    Installs:1,000,000+

    Download: Android, iOS

    3. Trading212

    Trading212 is one among the best 15 stock trading apps with a user-friendly interface. Initially, you can start with a free practice account with virtual money. This app contains hundreds of easy to use charts for analytics.

    It has educational programs on active trading practices. Multi-platform support, long term investing, market analysis are its marvellous features.

    App Features

    • Outstanding customer service
    • 2100+ CFDs on Stocks, Forex, Gold & more.
    • 24/7 CFDs on Cryptocurrencies
    • Direct trade executions on stock exchanges.

    Ratings: 4.5/5

    Installs: 5,000,000+

    Download: Android, iOS

    4. Acorns

    Acorns follow a simple sign-up procedure! So, start saving & invest your money seamlessly. You can invest for the future & choose the right plan for your retirement.

    Users get money from the top brands which they shop & spend in more savvy manner. The app also helps fresh investors through easy to study articles and contents from professional financial experts.

    App Features

    • Save money from your pay check.
    • Subscription tiers on different plans.
    • No minimum investments
    • All-in-one investment services.
    • Facile Direct investments for trading.

    Ratings: 4.5/5

    Installs:5,000,000+

    Download: Android, iOS

    5. Guru Trade7

    GuruTrade7 is a credible trading platform with the finest market assets. The well-designed platform leads people to engage in flexible trading.

    They have a technical support team to help clients 24/7. You can create a demo account where one can learn how to practice trade without any risks. GuruTrade7 assures maximum security on your funds by investing them in top tier banks.

    App Features

    • Multilingual customer support.
    • Stable trade system with major assets.
    • Real-time order execution.
    • Customization & flexible trading.

    Ratings: 4.5/5

    Installs: 1,000,000+

    Download: Android

    6. Capital.com

    Capital.com is an intuitive online trading app dedicated to traders. It enables users to elevate their trading strategy more successfully.

    One can study long term trends of stock markets & invest their money on the best one. You can start a free demo account & practice the supreme features including gold trading, bitcoin investing, etc.

    App Features

    • Services with high security & privacy.
    • Educational courses & trading guides for users.
    • Regular updates on stock markets.
    • Customizable interface.

    Ratings: 4.4/5

    Installs:1,000,000+

    Download: Android, iOS

    7. Olymp Trade

    Olymp trade provides an interactive trading experience on both Android & iOS. They have a team of world-class analysts who can help you in making risk-free trading operations. The invested funds are withdrawable at any time.

    Many webinars & tailored contents are devoted to users. The majority of features with a trader touch make it in the best 15 stock trading apps.

    App Features

    • User-friendly interface
    • No commissions
    • Monitoring efficient trade
    • Advance support by experts.

    Ratings: 4.3/5

    Installs: 50,000,000+

    Download: Android, iOS

    8. RobinHood

    Robinhood is one of the best trading apps with basic features. Whether you desire to buy funds, invest, or save your money, the app will show you a perfect path.

    Their trouble-free trading interface & tools makes everyone participate in effective trading services. The main objective of the app is to allow commission-free services for their users. You can customize your portfolio into a flexible one.

    App Features

    • Real-time trade execution.
    • Intuitive trading platform.
    • Fingerprint sensors for high security.
    • Convenient search function.

    Ratings: 4.3/5

    Installs: 10,000,000+

    Download: Android, iOS

    9. Groww

    Groww is Indias fastest online investment portal without any hidden charges. Like other apps, Groww offers you direct mutual funds. Traders can use Groww in multiple screens, whether in your mobiles or desktops.

    Groww ensures its seamless service for easy money management & safe investments of users. Open a Demat account & start trading in no time. Groww assures high-quality services by supporting all major banks.

    App Features

    • 100% online paperless accounts.
    • Know the latest financial insights.
    • Fast & secure trading.
    • Earn extra returns.

    Ratings: 4.3/5

    Installs: 5,000,000+

    Download: Android, iOS

    10. GOFOREX-Forex trading for beginners

    With GOFOREX beginners can enjoy learning the fundamentals of stock trading. They can prepare themselves to become a master Forex in no time. This app has rich content to engage more & more young trade enthusiasts into the app.

    GOFOREX comprises vivid illustrations & fun forex trading simulator games. Novice traders can grasp trading aspects through expert tips & friendly explanations on hard topics.

    App Features

    • Step-by-step guide for beginners.
    • Interactive quiz sessions
    • No signup required.
    • No third-party ads

    Ratings: 4.3/5

    Installs: 1,000,000+

    Download: Android, iOS

    11. StockEdge

    Stockedge is India’s best stock trading app with unbiased facts on stock markets for traders & investors. It is a package of fundamental, derivative & technical aspects of trading.

    You can have in-depth analysis & select reliable trading solutions according to their needs. The immersive interface itself makes it a must-have app to every trader for easy money management.

    App Features

    • Timely updates & technical scans on stock markets
    • Industry based study of stocks
    • Personalized watchlist & portfolios
    • Easy tracking of investments.

    Ratings: 4.3/5

    Installs: 1,000,000+

    Download: Android, iOS

    12. Kite by Zerodha

    Kite by Zerodha is a well-known trading platform with biometric access to the app. The most exciting feature of the app is that traders can make out trading in a lit backend dark mode.

    Zerodha has many other impressive apps like Varsity, Sentinel, and Coin. You can get instant status updates & push notifications on your orders. Zerodha gives you an array of embedded console reports on an innovative platform.

    App Features

    • Advanced charts & chart IQ.
    • Seamless login into all Zerodha apps.
    • Customizable watchlist for users.
    • Clean & super-fast UI.

    Ratings: 4.2/5

    Installs: 1,000,000+

    Download: Android, iOS

    13. Exness Trader

    Exness trader gains a place in one of the best 15 apps because of its exciting features. The app is a convenient, enjoyable & real-time trading platform giving you incredible investment service like no other.

    You can engage with faster order executions & withdrawal of money anytime anywhere. Users get a demo account automatically & access stock rates on the go. With Exness, you can have complete control over your account at ease.

    App Features

    • Push notifications about your orders & account activities.
    • Watchlist with your favorite assets
    • Instant deposits and withdrawal of funds
    • Candlesticks & line charts to analyze market trends

    Ratings: 4.2/5

    Installs: 1,000,000+

    Download: Android, iOS

    14. FBS Copytrade

    The name itself defines its dedicated area of service. Traders can gain benefits by copying trades of professional experts. Without any specific financial knowledge, traders can enter into the trading world with minimum deposits and gain maximum profit.

    This social learning app portrays a collection of detailed information on expert traders & even make you earn like them.

    App Features

    • Profile details of your favorite traders
    • 24/7 support of a technical team
    • Explore superior financial markets.
    • Real-time statistics of your investments
    • Intuitive & user-friendly interface.

    Ratings: 4.2/5

    Installs: 1,000,000+

    Download: Android, iOS

    15. Schwab Mobile

    Schwab mobiles give limitless access to your accounts, investments & latest news on stock markets. This app provides a safe account where you can login with fingerprint authentication and use their platinum Debit card service for instant purchases & real-time trading.

    App Features

    • Trade, edit or cancel your stock
    • Auto-updating watchlists.
    • Exclusive Schwab podcasts & videos
    • Well-designed UI.

    Ratings: 4.2/5

    Installs: 1,000,000+

    Download: Android, iOS
